Which field treatment feels right?

Same phrase — "ocean beach at sunset" — interpreted three ways. Each animates slowly, as it would in the app (real thing would be ~10× slower). Click to select.

A — Horizon bands

Soft horizontal strata, like a sunset horizon or a Rothko. The bands slowly drift and breathe. Strong sense of landscape — sky/light/ground reads instantly. Tuning gesture: drag the horizon up/down, tilt warmth.

B — Aperture glow

A luminous soft-edged pool of color breathing inside a darker surround — closest to Turrell's skyspaces and Ganzfeld pieces. Most "installation" of the three; frames beautifully behind ceramics on a plinth.

C — Breathing wash

Full-bleed diffuse color field with no structure — hues melt into and past each other, trading dominance over minutes. Most abstract and meditative; nothing to "read," only to feel. Closest to pure Ganzfeld.
